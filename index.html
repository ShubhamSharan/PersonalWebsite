About me

In 2021, I discovered my love for photography, sparked by the simple joy of riding my bike to new places. The journey has taken me to mossy mountains, vast farmlands, and frozen lakes. Since then, I have done professional photoshoots for weddings, portraits and the fan favorite of pet photos. I have been sharing my photography with the world through the open-source platform of Unsplash. Here, my images garner an audience of half a million views and 2.5K downloads each month.
